OpenID Sign-in or Create New Account

Why Login?

If you want to protect the features you created, you can login in with OpenID. Every feature you create will be connected with your email address. These features can only be edited and deleted when logged in with this account. Every feature that is created by an anonymous user can be edited or deleted by every other user and will not be safe.

What Information is used?

Spatial Viewer will use your OpenID account for identification. You can login with OpenID using your Google Account. For further information visit http://openid.net/get/. Spatial Viewer will only safe your email address if you create or edit a feature.

The email address you use will then be visible to every other user of the Spatial Viewer!
